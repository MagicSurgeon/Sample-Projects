About Pulse Heart

We Collaborate for Better Healthcare

Welcome to Pulse Heart Super Speciality Hospital, a leading healthcare institution committed to delivering excellence in medical care. Situated in the heart of Hyderabad, our advanced medical facility combines cutting-edge technology with a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals dedicated comprehensive and compassionate services.

At Pulse Heart, we specialize in cardiac care, offering a range of services from diagnostic procedures and non-invasive cardiology to advanced interventional cardiology and cardiac surgery. Our commitment extends beyond cardiology to include specialized departments in neurology, gastroenterology, gynecology, orthopedics, gynecology, Pediatrics, critical care medicine and general medicine.

We prioritize patient well-being, focusing on preventive care, accurate diagnoses, and tailored treatment plans.
